Discover the Majesty of Mount Rinjani

Rising 3,726 meters above sea level, Mount Rinjani is more than just a mountain—it is Indonesia’s most iconic trekking adventure. From the vast savannas of Sembalun to the breathtaking turquoise waters of Segara Anak Lake, every step offers a new wonder. Join us to conquer the summit of Lombok safely, accompanied by our experienced porters and the best mountain cuisine.

Your Gateway to Rinjani Adventure

This official website of Rinjani Dedytrekker is designed to simplify your trekking plans to Indonesia's second-highest volcano. Here, we provide comprehensive information on trekking routes, transparent package pricing, and an easy direct reservation system. We are dedicated to connecting adventurers with the best local porters and guides, ensuring your safety and comfort throughout the journey.
